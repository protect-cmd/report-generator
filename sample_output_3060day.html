Serving Instructions
HOW TO SERVE THIS NOTICE IN GEORGIA (O.C.G.A. § 44-7-7 — Best Practice): Step 1: Print three (3) copies of this notice. Step 2: CERTIFIED MAIL — Mail one copy via USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt Requested to the tenant at the rental property address. Keep the receipt and green card. Step 3: FIRST-CLASS MAIL — Mail a second copy via regular first-class mail on the same day. Step 4: DOOR POSTING — Post a third copy in a sealed envelope on the front door on the same day. Step 5: Document everything. Photograph the posted notice. Write down dates and tracking numbers. Step 6: Count sixty (60) CALENDAR DAYS from the date of service. Weekends and holidays are included. IMPORTANT: Accepting rent after serving this notice may constitute renewal of the tenancy and could invalidate this notice. Consult an attorney before accepting any payment.
