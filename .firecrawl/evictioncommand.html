Why Generic Forms Fail

Most Eviction Forms
Get Cases Thrown Out.

Three mistakes landlords make that cost them months of lost rent — and how Eviction Command eliminates all of them.

01
Wrong Notice Period

Eviction notice periods vary by county and violation type. A 3-day notice in one county may require different language than the same notice in an adjacent county. One day off and the judge dismisses your case.

Typical cost of dismissal 60-90 days lost
02
Missing Legal Language

State law requires specific disclosures and wording in every eviction notice. Generic templates downloaded from the internet routinely miss required language under current law — especially after recent legislative changes.

Average lost rent $3,000 – $6,000
03
Wrong Service Method

How and where you deliver the notice has specific legal requirements in every state. Personal delivery, door posting, certified mail — each has rules. Serve it wrong and the notice is legally invalid regardless of the content.
