GEORGIA LEGAL REQUIREMENTS — 3-DAY NOTICE TO PAY OR VACATE:
- Governing statute: O.C.G.A. § 44-7-50(c), as amended by 2024 Ga. Laws Act 392 (HB 404), effective 7/1/2024
- Notice period: THREE (3) BUSINESS DAYS. Weekends (Saturday, Sunday) and legal holidays do NOT count. Count only business days when calculating the deadline.
- Amount that may be demanded: All past due rent, late fees, utilities, and other charges owed as of the date of notice. Do NOT add amounts not in the client data.
- Required elements: Landlord name, tenant name(s), property address, exact total amount itemized by category, three-business-day deadline, and date of notice.
- Late fees and utilities may be included per the 2024 statute. Attorney fees are not required.
